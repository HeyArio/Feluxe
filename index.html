About Feluxe Cat Litter

Excellence in Vertical Integration

Feluxe Cat Litter is a leading manufacturer of premium clumping cat litter in Iran, proudly offering PETOPIA for the domestic market and FELUXE for international markets. As part of a specialized industrial group, we manage the entire value chain through our own subsidiary company.

From locating and extracting the finest bentonite mines in Iran, to micronizing and manufacturing - this vertical integration ensures consistent and reliable quality with competitive pricing through cost-efficient production. Every step from mining to packaging is carried out entirely within our factory, with no outsourcing.

Our Unique Advantage

SPS is the ONLY cat litter factory in Iran with Iran Veterinary Organization License. This exclusive certification, maintained through regular inspections, ensures compliance and safety that no other Iranian manufacturer can match.

In addition to our veterinary certification, we maintain ISO 9001 Certification and adherence to international standards, ensuring our products meet global quality requirements for export markets worldwide.

Our domestic brand PETOPIA is the best-selling cat litter brand in Iran, distributed nationwide across all provinces and trusted by veterinarians, pet shops, and cat owners for unmatched quality.
